Who We Are

Nurturing Nature, Cultivating Passion At Litle Forest, we believe bonsai is not just a plant—it’s a philosophy, a journey of resilience and beauty. Founded with the dream of connecting passionate bonsai artists with nature lovers, we provide a platform to showcase and celebrate this living art form. Every tree carries the soul of its creator, and here, we honor that connection.

Handcrafted by Nature, Perfected by Passion Discover a collection of meticulously nurtured bonsai trees, each with its own character and charm. From delicate miniatures to majestic old-world species, every bonsai in our collection is a masterpiece. Browse, find your perfect tree, and bring home a piece of timeless beauty.

Mission
  • To connect bonsai artists with nature lovers by providing a platform to showcase, sell, and celebrate the art of bonsai.
  • We aim to make bonsai accessible to everyone while supporting the artists who nurture these living masterpieces.
Vision
  • To be the world’s leading online marketplace for bonsai, where artistry meets commerce, and every tree finds a home.
  • We envision a global community that appreciates and preserves the timeless beauty of bonsai.

Meet Our Founder - Sagar Varma

Sagar Varma is a creative visionary and a passionate nature lover who finds solace and inspiration in the delicate art of bonsai. With a thriving career in the creative industry, Sagar has always been captivated by the harmony and artistry involved in crafting bonsai. His love for nature and his fascination with the meticulous process of shaping and nurturing miniature trees led him to envision Litle Forest — a platform dedicated to bonsai artistry.

Having spent years observing the dedication of bonsai artists, Sagar realized the need for a space where these creators could showcase their work and connect with a global audience. Litle Forest is the culmination of his dream to bridge the gap between bonsai artists and enthusiasts, offering a place to celebrate, buy, and learn about these living works of art.

When he’s not immersed in his professional endeavors, Sagar can often be found tending to his own bonsai collection or exploring the natural world for inspiration. Through Little Forest, he hopes to inspire others to discover the serenity and joy that bonsai brings, fostering a deeper connection to nature and the art it inspires.
